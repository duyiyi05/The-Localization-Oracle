## What Is This?

The Localization Oracle is an interactive tarot-inspired portfolio project that maps the nine core disciplines of Localization Project Management onto the Major Arcana.

Each card flips to reveal a real localization PM concept — complete with its tarot correspondence, an oracle reading, a professional definition, and a real-life scenario showing what happens when the card is heeded... and when it is ignored.

It is equal parts portfolio, educational tool, and creative experiment.

---

## The Nine Cards

| Arcana | Tarot Card | Localization Discipline |
|--------|-----------|------------------------|
| I | The Magician | Translation Memory & TM Leverage |
| II | The High Priestess | Linguistic Quality Assurance (LQA) |
| III | The Fool | Pseudotranslation |
| IV | The Chariot | Machine Translation Post-Editing (MTPE) |
| V | The Hermit | Fuzzy Match Thresholds & Word Count Analysis |
| VI | The Empress | Linguistic Asset Management |
| VII | The Hierophant | Vendor Management & LSP Briefing |
| VIII | Justice | Localization Testing (In-context Review) |
| IX | The World | Cultural Adaptation & Transcreation |

## Features

- **9 interactive tarot cards** — click to flip, each revealing a localization PM concept
- **Real Rider-Waite card imagery** (public domain, 1909) styled to the dark witchy aesthetic
- **Tarot correspondence** — every card maps to a specific Major Arcana with explained symbolism
- **Oracle language** — all explanations written in witchy atmospheric prose before the professional definition
- **Real-life example modal** — every card has a real company scenario, heeded/ignored outcome split, and PM wisdom note
- **Three-stage spread layout** — Linguistic Arts → Technical Process → Management & Delivery
- **Animated star field** background with gold accent stars
- **Responsive** — works on mobile and desktop
- **SVG fallback** — illustrated SVG art appears automatically if images fail to load
